(1.1.0) Project Summary

Stockeye is a web application that aggregates and analyses tweets that are related to the market from the popular social networking service Twitter, and offers information regarding the public sentiments of stocks to its users. StockEye seeks to serve its audience in multiple ways. For one, using Twitter as a source of data is valuable due to the impact that public opinion has on the market. This enables users to make more informed investment decisions. Additionally, this platform will save users valuable time, as they will no longer be required to research and analyse the Twitter sentiments themselves.
